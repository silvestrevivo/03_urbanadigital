Activity from 2002 till 2015

Silvestre Vivo

Architect, photograph & UrbanDesigner

Urbana Digital is a personal project which starts on 2009 in Spain after a few years of experience in partnership, and a considerable amount of architectural projects and competitions developed from 2003 till 2009.

The back to the University on that year to extend the knowledge about sustainability and energy applied on the architectural field, allowed me to write my first research project and lectures.

Currently I am based in The Netherlands, which is improving the experience about a new vision over the profession and the activity as architect, besides helping to the internationalization of the office.
